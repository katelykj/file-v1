How to make Mama Johnson's Osso Buco


Ingredients

  • 2T flour
  • 1/2tsp pepper
  • 1/4tsp salt
  • 4 veal shanks
  • 2T olive oil
  • 1C carrot (mincied)
  • 1C celery (minced)
  • 1C onion (minced)
  • 1C white wine
  • 1 large garlic clove (minced)
  • 1 can tomato palpa
  • 1/2C beef broth
  • 2tsp chopped fresh rosemary
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Polenta

Directions

Step 1

Preheat oven to 350F


Step 2

Combine flour, salt, and pepper in a Ziplock bag. Add veal shanks to flour mixture. Toss to coat.


Step 3

Heat oil in large Dutch oven over mid-high heat. Add veal and brown on each side for about 3 mins. Set aside.


Step 4

Reduce heat to medium; add carrot, celery, onion garlic and wine. Cook 5 minsutes.


Step 5

Return veal to pan, add tomatoes, broth and herbs. Cover pan with heavy aluminum foil, then lid. Bake for 2+ hours until veal is tender. Serve veal and sauce over warm polenta.


Enjoy!
